# Modality-Native Routing (MMA2A)

The MMA2A protocol eliminates the 'text-bottleneck' in Agent-to-Agent communication by routing multimodal signals in their native form.

- **Key Gain**: +20pp Task Completion Accuracy (TCA) on CrossModal-CS.
- **Critical Dependency**: requires capable multimodal reasoning agents downstream.
- **Trade-off**: 1.8x latency increase.

Reference: [arXiv:2604.12213](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.12213)
